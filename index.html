Inobi Smart Public Transport

Inobi as a step towards the Smart City

Inobi Smart Public Transport is a unique combination of proprietary hardware, software and firmware tailor-made to deliver multiple technological solutions. Our complete smart package solution provides the necessary tools and allows to monitor, control and enhance operation of transport operators based on Real Time Data and smart reports.

  • GPS module
  • Wi-Fi
  • Speaker Module
  • Passenger counter
  • ABD reader
  • Video output
